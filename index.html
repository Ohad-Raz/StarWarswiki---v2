What Is The StarWars Saga

The Star Wars saga is an epic science fiction franchise created by George Lucas. It revolves around the battle between the Jedi, defenders of peace and justice, and the Sith, followers of the dark side of the Force. Spanning multiple trilogies, it features iconic characters like Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, and Princess Leia, set in a galaxy filled with starships, lightsabers, and epic interstellar conflicts.
